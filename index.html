About The Firm

Your Trusted Financial and Compliance Partner

Founded in 1997 by CA Prakash M. Oswal, our firm has grown into a trusted Chartered Accountancy practice with a dedicated team of 30+ employees. For three decades, we have built our reputation on integrity, technical excellence, and long-term client relationships.

We provide comprehensive services across Audit, Taxation, Litigation, and Assurance—strengthening businesses with robust compliance and financial discipline. As business environments evolve, so do we. Our expanding capabilities in Virtual CFO, Advisory, and Internal Audit enable us to support clients not just in meeting regulatory requirements, but in driving performance, managing risk, and achieving sustainable growth.

Client-focused at our core, we combine deep technical rigor with personalized guidance—ensuring clarity in numbers, confidence in decisions, and consistency in execution.

Today, we stand as a full-spectrum financial partner, blending legacy expertise with forward-thinking financial leadership.

Services

What we do

Audit & Assurance

Our Audit and Assurance services are designed to enhance financial credibility, strengthen internal controls, and ensure regulatory compliance. We conduct statutory audits, tax audits and other assurance engagements with a risk-based and detail-oriented approach. By combining technical expertise with practical insights, we help businesses improve transparency, identify operational gaps, and build stakeholder confidence.

Taxation, Compliance & Litigation

Our Taxation and Litigation services help businesses navigate complex tax regulations with clarity and confidence. We provide comprehensive support in direct and indirect taxation, including advisory, compliance, assessments, and representation before tax authorities. With a strategic and detail-oriented approach, we assist clients in minimizing risks, resolving disputes efficiently, and ensuring sustained regulatory compliance.

Internal Audit

Our Internal Audit services focus on strengthening governance, enhancing risk management, and improving operational efficiency. Through a systematic and risk-based approach, we evaluate internal controls, identify process gaps, and recommend practical improvements. Beyond compliance, we aim to add value by helping management build stronger systems, reduce exposure to risk, and support informed decision-making.

Virtual CFO

Our Virtual CFO services provide businesses with strategic financial leadership without the cost of a full-time CFO. We support budgeting, forecasting, MIS reporting, cash flow management, financial planning, and investor readiness. By combining strong financial discipline with forward-looking insights, we help management make informed decisions, improve profitability, and drive sustainable growth.

Accounting and Bookkeeping

Our Accounting and Bookkeeping services ensure accurate, timely, and compliant financial records. We manage day-to-day transactions, ledger maintenance, reconciliations, financial statement preparation, and statutory compliance. With a structured and technology-driven approach, we provide reliable financial data that supports informed decision-making and smooth business operations.

Ancillary Services

Business Formation & Structuring – Assistance with entity incorporation, registrations, and optimal structuring for tax and regulatory efficiency.

Compliance Management – Ongoing support for ROC filings, corporate law compliance, and regulatory reporting.

Financial Due Diligence – Independent financial reviews for investments, acquisitions, and strategic transactions.

Project Reports & CMA Data – Preparation of financial projections and reports for bank financing and credit facilities.

Management Reporting (MIS) – Customized reporting frameworks to improve monitoring and decision-making.

Process & Control Advisory – Designing and strengthening internal processes for improved governance and efficiency.
